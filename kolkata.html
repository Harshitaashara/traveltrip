TOP PLACES TO VISIT IN WEST BENGAL


KOLKATA– City Of Joy

Kolkata is India’s second-largest city and the face of West Bengal. It is an interminably progressing celebration of human existence. This dynamic 350-year-old city situated on India’s Eastern Coast is the capital of West Bengal
Ideal duration: 4-5 days
Places to visit : Victoria Memorial, Howrah bridge, Eco Park, Mother's Wax Museum,etc.


DARJEELING– Himalayan Jewel

If one wants to witness the stunning snow pinnacles of Kanchenjunga, the wonderful tea cultivations, the sweet-smelling orchids, the greenery of the pines, and the adorable Himalayan toy train making its way through the dazzling mountains, head to the city of Darjeeling
Best Time to Visit Pondicherry: March to April
Ideal duration:4-7 days
Places to visit in Darjeeling: Tea plantation gardens, handmade artifacts at the New Mahakal Market, and Tibetian delicacies.


SUNDARBANS– Land Of Mangroves

This UNESCO World Heritage Site is the biggest mangrove eco-arrangement in the world. Home to one of India’s most notorious untamed life species, the Royal Bengal Tiger, it is also the world’s biggest estuarine woods befuddled by many streams and tributaries.
Ideal duration: 3-4 days
Places to visit in Sundarbans: White tigers, trekking, and exploration of India’s pristine natural reserve.


KALIMPONG– Blend Of Cultures

This melting pot of cultures from the Indians, British, Nepalese, and Tibetans is situated 3 hours away from Darjeeling. Its flowering gardens, Buddhist monasteries, historic churches, and sacred temples make it a renowned tourist destination.
Ideal duration: 2 days
Places to visit in Kalimpong: Delo park, Pine View Nursery, Science centre, Mungsong


PURULIA – A Scenic Drive

Encompassing lush greenery, panoramic views, and mesmerizing hills, Purulia is adorned with scenic hills. This place can be explored to relish the verdant landscapes of West Bengal and experience pure serenity.
Ideal duration: 2 days
Places to visit in Purulia: Joychandi Pahar,Ajodhya Hills,Saheb Bandh
