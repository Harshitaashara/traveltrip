TOP PLACES TO VISIT IN TAMIL NADU


CHENNAI– Cultural Centre

Chennai, on the Bay of Bengal in eastern India, is the capital of the state of Tamil Nadu.
Best Time to Visit Chennai: November to February
Ideal duration: 3-4 days
Places to visit : Marina Beach, Government Museum, Fort St. George, Edward Elliot’s Beach, Ashtalakshmi Temple, Mylapore, Valluvar Kottam


PONDICHERRY– French Colony

Also known as Puducherry, this is an ideal place for explorers, beach lovers, and experience-seekers interested in French culture and architecture.
Best Time to Visit Pondicherry: October to March
Ideal duration: 4 days
Places to visit in Pondicherry: Sri Aurobindo Ashram, Promendade Beach, Arulmigu Manakula, Basilica of the Sacred Heart, Paradise Beach, Auroville


MUDUMALAI– Ancient Hills

Located in the Nilgiri hills, Mudumalai is one of the best tourist places in Tamil Nadu, which is known for its wildlife persona.
Best Time to Visit Mudumalai:October to May
Ideal duration: 2-3 days
Places to visit in Mudumalai: Bandipur Tiger Reserve, Madumalai National Park, Gopalswami Hills, Needle Rock View Point


HOGENAKKAL– Gorgeous Waterfalls

Situated in Dharampuri, this small village is an absolute hotspot for romantic getaways, a short family vacay, and even an offbeat trip with your friends.
Best Time to Visit Hogenakkal:September to March
Ideal duration: 2 days
Places to visit in Hogenakkal: Hogenakkal Waterfalls, Hanging Bridge, Crocodile Park, Hogenakkal Falls View Point


TUTICORIN – Stunning Beaches

From boasting a beautiful beach, a spectacular wildlife sanctuary to numerous temples and churches, Tuticorin is one of the best tourist places in Tamil Nadu that makes sure that every moment of your holiday outshines the previous one.
Best Time to Visit Tuticorin: November to March
Ideal duration: 2-3 days
Places to visit in Tuticorin: Baisilica of Our Lady of Snows, Hare Island, Muthu Nagar Beach, Thiruchendur Murugan Temple
